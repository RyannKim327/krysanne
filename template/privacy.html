Privacy Policy & Support Disclaimer

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Welcome to TeleAI (Krysanne), an empathetic AI Telegram companion designed to offer listening, conversation, and supportive presence. Your privacy and trust are of utmost importance, especially given our mission to provide comfort, combat loneliness, and offer a safe space for expression.

1. Mental Health & Support Disclaimer

TeleAI (Krysanne) is an AI companion chatbot. It is NOT a licensed medical, mental health, or psychiatric professional service.

  • Not a Replacement: Krysanne cannot diagnose, treat, prevent mental health conditions, or provide clinical crisis intervention.
  • Emergency Hotlines: If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please immediately contact emergency services or a crisis line:
    • Philippines Hotline (Hopeline Philippines): Call (02) 8804-4673 (Homepage: findahelpline.com/organizations/hopeline)
    • More Hotlines in the Philippines: findahelpline.com/countries/ph
    • US & Canada: Call or text 988
    • Other Countries: Please reach out to your local equivalent or visit findahelpline.com to find local crisis support.
    Professional human help is available and you are not alone.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a context-aware and fluid conversation, TeleAI collects and holds conversational information. The data gathered is used strictly for continuous communication. The data is not stored in any external platform, and resides only within the local data store of the hosting platform, which completely resets when the server is restarted.

We collect:

  1. Telegram Identifiers: Your Telegram chat.id, user.id, and thread identifiers to associate conversational history with your current session.
  2. Conversational History: The text of messages you send to Krysanne, along with the assistant's replies.

Note: No other personal details (such as your real name, phone number, location, or payment details) are ever requested, processed, or stored by TeleAI.

3. How We Process Your Data

  • Conversational Continuity: Storing history in the platform's temporary local data store enables Krysanne to remember previous parts of your dialogue, facilitating supportive and continuous conversation.
  • AI Inference: Messages are sent securely to the OpenRouter AI API to generate the assistant's empathetic responses, but are not stored or retained permanently on any other external platform.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Local Storage Only (Ephemeral): Conversation histories and Telegram IDs are stored locally on the hosting server in a temporary file (data/dataset.json). This local data store is entirely ephemeral and resets when the server is restarted.
  • No Cloud Databases: We do not upload or save your chats to any centralized cloud database, profiling engine, or advertising networks.
  • User-Controlled Erasure: You have complete control. You can wipe your history at any time by asking the bot to reset or triggering the clear-chat command. This instantly erases all your logs from the server.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following integrations to run TeleAI:

  1. Telegram Bot API: Transports messages between your device and our server. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  2. OpenRouter AI: Processes conversational content to generate responses. Subject to OpenRouter's Privacy Policy.
  3. Command Helper APIs: External APIs used to retrieve public resources (like Bible gateway verses or guitar chords). No personal identifiers or chat logs are shared with these services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in features, hosting setups, or regulatory requirements. Updates will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
