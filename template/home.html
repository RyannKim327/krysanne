An empathetic, always-on AI companion hosted on Telegram. Designed to listen, understand, and provide a supportive presence whenever you need it.

Our Mission

“What I aim is to give people a friend, to prevent suicide cases. To call someone who listens to them.”

In an increasingly connected world, isolation and mental health challenges continue to grow. TeleAI was built to address this gap by offering a non-judgmental, warm, and highly supportive conversational presence. While not a replacement for human relationship or professional medical support, Krysanne is always ready to offer a listening ear.

Empathetic Conversations

Trained to respond with warmth, comfort, and care. Krysanne is here to support you during difficult times without any judgment.

Privacy by Design

Conversations are stored locally on the server. You are in complete control: simply clear your history to erase all data instantly.

Extensible Commands

Integrates with Ultimate Guitar for chords, Bible Gateway for scriptures, and Lumenfall AI for on-the-fly image generation.

IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTICE

TeleAI is an AI-powered companion and not a professional mental health service. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing severe crisis, please reach out to professional emergency services or your local national crisis lifeline (such as (02) 8804-4673 (Hopeline Philippines; Homepage: findahelpline.com/organizations/hopeline) or 988 in the US and Canada). For more local hotlines, visit findahelpline.com/countries/ph. Professional help is always available.
